About Design Interforms

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Design Interforms began as a small furniture workshop in Nairobi, Kenya. Over the years, we've evolved into a comprehensive interior design and installation company serving both residential and commercial clients across the country.

Our journey started with custom furniture manufacturing, and we've expanded our expertise to include complete interior solutions, from kitchen and wardrobe fittings to commercial space design and gypsum ceiling installations.

Our Mission

To transform spaces through innovative design, quality craftsmanship, and exceptional service, creating functional and beautiful environments that enhance our clients' lives and businesses.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's leading interior design and installation company, recognized for excellence in craftsmanship, innovative design solutions, and outstanding customer service.
